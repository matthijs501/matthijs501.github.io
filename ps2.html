The playstation 2, the second generation gameconsole from playstation.

This console was a even bigger hit than the PS1, because the spec are a lot better.

One of the unique features of the PS2 was that users could sit it horizontally or vertically depending of their preferance.

PS2 offered games the option to play online for free with gamers from around the world. the memory card for the PS2 was only 8MB
